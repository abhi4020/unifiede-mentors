Frequently Asked Questions

What are your gym hours?

We're open 24/7 for members. Regular classes run from 6 AM to 10 PM. Visit our facilities anytime that works for you!

Do I need experience to join?

No experience necessary! Our trainers provide guidance for all fitness levels. We have beginner, intermediate, and advanced classes.

Is there a contract requirement?

We offer flexible membership options with no long-term contracts required. You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice.

Can I book a personal trainer?

Yes! Visit our Trainers page to view all certified trainers, their specialties, and book sessions directly through our platform.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, UPI, and bank transfers. Monthly subscriptions are convenient and secure.

Is there a free trial available?

Absolutely! We offer a 7-day free trial for new members. No credit card required. Come experience our facilities risk-free!

Do you provide nutrition guidance?

Yes, our trainers and nutrition specialists provide personalized dietary guidance as part of Premium and Elite memberships.

What if I can't attend a class?

Class cancellations can be done through your member dashboard up to 24 hours before. No penalties apply for timely cancellations.
